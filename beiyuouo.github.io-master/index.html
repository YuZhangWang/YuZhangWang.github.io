Biography

I am a postgraduate student in Computer Science @Institute of Computing Technology, Chinese Academy of Sciences, under the supervision of Prof. Yiqiang Chen.

My current research interests focus on federated learning, edge computing, and distributed systems.

Please feel free to send me an email, if you have any questions. I'd like to communicate with you.

个人简介

我本科在海南大学软件工程专业就读，现在是 @中科院计算所 的一名在读硕士生。

我的研究兴趣主要集中在联邦学习、边缘计算和分布式系统。

如果您有任何问题可以直接发邮件给我，欢迎与我交流，我非常乐意和你交朋友。
